=== How to read UW ratings on RMP ===
- For large intro STEM courses (e.g., MATH 124 calculus, CHEM 142 general chemistry),
  the lecturer varies by quarter, so search the specific instructor's name rather than the
  course number. The quiz/lab-section TA often matters as much as the lecturer for your grade.
- RMP skews toward strong opinions (very positive or very negative). Triangulate with the
  UW grade-distribution tool (DAWGPath) and recent student discussion before deciding.
- Browse all UW professors: https://www.ratemyprofessors.com/search/professors/1530?q=*

=== Common themes across UW reviews ===
- "Show up to lectures" and "go to office hours" recur in positive reviews.
- Project/assignment grading in CSE is described as strict but predictable ("style requirements").
- Difficulty ratings cluster around 3.3–3.5 even for well-liked intro instructors — these are
  fast 10-week courses.
