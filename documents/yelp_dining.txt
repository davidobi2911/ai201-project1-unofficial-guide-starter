=== Thai Tom — Thai ===
Address: 4543 University Way NE, Seattle, WA 98105
Yelp: ~2,556 reviews (1,160+ photos) — https://www.yelp.com/biz/thai-tom-seattle
A legendary, tiny, cash-only Thai spot. Spicy wok cooking done at a counter you can watch; bar
seating next to the kitchen. No-frills room, student favorite, most menu items around/under $8.
Reviewers warn the heat levels are no joke — don't try to be a spice hero. Expect a line.
(Also listed on Tripadvisor: https://www.tripadvisor.com/Restaurant_Review-g60878-d480123-Reviews-Thai_Tom-Seattle_Washington.html)

=== Aladdin Gyro-cery — Greek / Mediterranean ===
Address: 4139 University Way NE, Seattle, WA 98105
Yelp: 673 reviews (312+ photos) — https://www.yelp.com/biz/aladdin-gyro-cery-seattle
Frequently called the biggest portions and most affordable food on the Ave. Reviewers praise the
crispy, flavorful falafel with tahini sauce, the gyros, and the garlic-and-feta "Greek fries."
Open late — popular post-library/post-party food. Common complaint: it's usually packed (expect a
line), and service can be inconsistent.
(Review: https://www.theinfatuation.com/seattle/reviews/aladdin-gyro-cery)

=== Shawarma King — Middle Eastern ===
Address: 4337 University Way NE, Seattle, WA 98105 (established 2008)
Yelp: 387 reviews (241+ photos) — https://www.yelp.com/biz/shawarma-king-seattle-7
Quick, cheap, generous portions — a U-District institution for hungry students. Popular for
late-night dinner. Menu includes shawarma, falafel, hummus, and Greek fries. Reviews repeatedly
mention friendly, patient, fast service and a good atmosphere.

=== Cafe Allegro — Coffee ===
Address: 4214 University Way NE, Seattle, WA 98105
Yelp: 304 reviews (223+ photos) — https://www.yelp.com/biz/cafe-allegro-seattle
Tucked in a former mortuary garage off the Ave, Allegro is recognized as the first cafe in Seattle
to specialize in espresso. Reviewers single out the excellent, expertly made coffee and efficient
staff; it's a genuine study spot rather than a scene. Rated 4.0/5 on Tripadvisor.
(Tripadvisor: https://www.tripadvisor.com/Restaurant_Review-g60878-d432211-Reviews-Cafe_Allegro-Seattle_Washington.html)

=== Tips from reviews ===
- Thai Tom and Aladdin Gyro-cery are the two most-mentioned student favorites on the Ave.
- Several of these spots are cash-friendly and most meals run well under $10.
- Expect lines at the popular places, especially Thai Tom and Aladdin, around lunch and late night.
- On-campus dining (Local Point, Center Table, the HUB, District Market) takes Husky Card dining
  dollars; the Ave generally does not.
