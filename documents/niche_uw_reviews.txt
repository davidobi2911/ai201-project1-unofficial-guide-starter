=== Niche Ratings (June 2026) ===
- Overall Niche grade: A+
- Student-review star rating: 3.82 / 5, based on 4,891 reviews.
- UW is consistently ranked by Niche among the top public universities in Washington.

=== What reviewers praise ===
- Knowledgeable, research-active professors who bring their research into teaching.
- A diverse student body and a very active extracurricular scene — Niche notes UW has over
  800 Registered Student Organizations (RSOs), spanning interests from anime to programming
  to the outdoors.
- A lively campus culture, with Husky football and other athletics contributing to school spirit.

=== What reviewers criticize ===
- High cost of living in Seattle.
- Large class sizes, especially in introductory courses, which can make personal attention hard.
- A competitive, sometimes impersonal environment — particularly around "impacted"
  (capacity-constrained) majors, which several reviewers describe as high-stress.

=== Recurring themes ===
- UW is described as a great institution for students willing to be proactive and navigate a
  big, research-heavy university.
- Strengths: research opportunities, Seattle location/tech proximity, club scene, school spirit.
- Trade-offs: big intro lectures, cost of living, and competition to get into selective majors.

Note: Star rating and review count above are the aggregate figures shown on the Niche reviews
page as of June 2026 and will drift over time as new reviews are added.
